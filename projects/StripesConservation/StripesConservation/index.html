Our Mission

Help us keep these beautiful cretures alive.

We here at Stipes Conservation have made it our mission to make the preservation of these beautiful tigers, who have been on the decline since the 70's, our top priority. We hope to have these amazing animals around for generations after we are gone, however the only way to do that we need the generous donations from people and those who volenteer to help rescue and care for our rehabilitating tigers.

If you are intrested in protecting these majestic creatures and prolonging there existiance, we ask that you please support our cause by subscribing to our weekly newsletter.
